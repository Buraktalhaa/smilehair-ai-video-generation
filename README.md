This repository contains the production process and outputs of the **AI Powered Video Generation** project, which won **2nd Place** in the Smile Hair Clinic AI & Mobile Hackathon.

Our project combines AI technology with a compelling narrative, emphasizing that hair loss is not just a physical change but a **universal psychological burden** for many individuals. We positioned artificial intelligence not as the end goal, but as a **powerful tool to amplify the story.**

## ğŸ“– The Story â€“ Brief Summary
We created a narrative following the internal journeys of three characters experiencing hair loss, caught between societal pressure and loss of self-confidence. The story is structured into 3 parts:

1. **The Concealment:** The moment the character notices the change and the feeling of helplessness.
2. **The Confrontation:** Societal stares and internal monologues.
3. **The Transformation:** Acceptance, seeking solutions, and regaining self-confidence.

Every scene was generated using AI with a focus on consistent facial expressions, color palettes, and environmental atmospheres.
